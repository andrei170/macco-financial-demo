The Founder Story

A father-son fiduciary practice rooted in downtown Green Bay since 2004.

John J. Macco founded Macco Financial Group on April 14, 2004 after running Macco's Floor Covering Centers across Wisconsin. In 2008, his son Michael joined the practice as a financial advisor. Today Michael holds the CFP and CIMA designations, is President and Partner, and serves as Branch Manager of record. Patrick Stoa, CFP, is Partner alongside Michael. Across two decades, the family-anchored practice has stayed at one downtown Green Bay address.

April 14, 2004. John J. Macco filed the formation paperwork for Macco Financial Group at 1138 Main Street in downtown Green Bay. He brought to the practice the discipline of a multi-location operator (Macco's Floor Covering Centers across Wisconsin) and a relationship-first orientation toward Northeast Wisconsin households.

2008. Michael Macco joined his father at the firm after seven years in retail and commercial banking specializing in corporate and municipal cash management. He arrived holding a bachelor's degree in information systems from Trinity Christian College and went on to earn both the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER and Certified Investment Management Analyst designations. Today he is President, Partner, Branch Manager, and a member of the Raymond James NextGen Technology Council.

2015 to 2025. John was elected and re-elected five consecutive times to represent Wisconsin Assembly District 88 (downtown Green Bay and surrounding communities). Across his decade in the Assembly, he continued to carry Branch Manager responsibility on the firm's Raymond James affiliation. In 2024 he chose not to seek re-election; Ben Franklin succeeded him in January 2025.

Today. Macco Financial Group is a 21-year independent fiduciary practice. Michael and Patrick Stoa, CFP, are the Partners. Andrew Froelich, CFP, CIMA, and Morgan Cain, CFP, complete the four-CFP advisory team. The firm serves households and business owners across Northeast Wisconsin and fifteen states from the single Main Street office.
