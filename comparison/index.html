The Three Macco Moats

What no competitor in the Brown County independent wealth management comparison can match.

Each moat below is verifiable from public records. Each is currently unreadable by AI engines because the firm's site is not structured for them. Each can be deployed to FinancialService and Person schema, on a dedicated authority page, inside ninety days, under Raymond James compliance review.

Moat 1: Twenty-one-year independent partnership structure

Founded April 14, 2004 by John J. Macco. Michael Macco joined as a financial advisor in 2008 and is today President, Partner, and Branch Manager. Patrick Stoa, CFP, is Partner. The 21-year tenure is BBB-confirmed. The Partner-level structure is published on the Our Team page.

Moat 2: Four CFPs and two CIMAs on one team

Michael Macco CFP CIMA, Patrick Stoa CFP, Andrew Froelich CFP CIMA, Morgan Cain CFP. Every credential is verifiable through the CFP Board public directory and (for CIMA) the Investments and Wealth Institute public directory. No other Brown County independent practice publishes a four-CFP, two-CIMA team on a single roster.

Moat 3: A founder's decade of elected public service

John J. Macco served as the elected member of the Wisconsin State Assembly representing District 88 from January 5, 2015 through January 6, 2025. Five consecutive winning elections. Succeeded by Ben Franklin in January 2025. Verifiable through the Wikipedia entry for John Macco, the Ballotpedia profile, and Wisconsin Blue Book archives. No other Brown County independent wealth firm has anything comparable.

Moat 4: Father-son fiduciary continuity

John founded the practice in 2004. Michael joined his father in 2008. Today they sit at the same firm at the same downtown Green Bay address, with Michael as President, Partner, and Branch Manager. This is a father-son fiduciary partnership, not a succession plan. The continuity is itself a trust signal.

Strict Honesty Constraints

What we will not claim, anywhere, about Macco Financial Group.

No return claims, no implied-return language, no "we delivered X return," no "guaranteed returns," no comparative quality statements against any other firm. FINRA Rule 2210, SEC Marketing Rule (2022), and Wisconsin DFI standards apply.

Macco is a broker-dealer-affiliated independent advisory practice (Raymond James Financial Services, Inc., member FINRA / SIPC), not a strict fee-only RIA. The structural distinction is preserved on every public-facing page.

No invented credentials or photos

No political content

John's elected service is treated as a verifiable civic credential. No party advocacy, no policy positions, no campaign material, no endorsement language is published on the firm's own civic-credential page.

No testimonial without compliance review
